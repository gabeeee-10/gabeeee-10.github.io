What is Broadcast Journalism?

A program that focuses on the methods and techniques for reporting, producing, and delivering news and news programs via radio, television, and video/film media; and that prepares individuals to be professional broadcast journalists, editors, producers, directors, and managers.

My Interest in Broadcast Journalism

I love sports. I have my entire life. I love the statistics, the analyzation, the competition, play-style and much more. I have realized that playing the game proffessionally might not be in the cards for me, but I also realize the knowledge that I have about the game could still keep me around the game, just watching from right above the field. Broadcast Journalism is any kind of joiurnalism on television, radio, social media, etc. With the knowledge and the ability I have to break down games, I believe this goal of mine is very achievable.
