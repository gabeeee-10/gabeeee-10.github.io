Why I love Traveling

The average person's life in years in the United States is around 70 years. During those 70 years, about one third is spent sleeping. ANother one third is spent in a school or work environment. For the extra one third, I want to travel the world as much as I can. Every summer, me and my dad go on summer trips around the world. We usually visit 1-3 new countries and visit Israel, where my dad was born for around 2 weeks in length. I have been to a total of 20 countries with my dad, and I love every single one of them. I have been to world class cities like Tokyo, Barcelona, Rome, and Paris.

Aspects of Traveling I enjoy

  • The Worldwide Cuisines
  • Going to Different Airports and Lounges
  • Seeing Monuments, different kinds of Architecture.
  • Making Memories
  • New Hotels, views, and Sunsets.
